About Ambiora

Started in 2007, Ambiora, the techfest of NMIMS Shirpur was marked as the fastest growing fest in India. The innovations by the great minds of Ambiora have put Shirpur on the technical map of India.

In the year 2018 Ambiora had a record footfall of 5200 people, which was 25% more than the previous edition. Ambiora brings experts and students of different backgrounds together to create a competitive ambience in our college.

The annual fest of the college is unarguably the most awaited event of the entire year which is known for its quality, accountability and attractiveness.

“Ambiora” boosts out its own record of number of events organized and participants that take part in the competitions every year.

Rahul Tyagi is a post-graduate with majors in Computer Science and has conducted to his account, more than a hundred training sessions globally in past 5 years. Being a cyber space researcher himself, Rahul can claim to have found critical vulnerabilities on websites of Intel, Sony, HP, Discovery Networks, TED and many more. He is also listed in BlackBerry Hall of Fame for his contributions to the organization. A Published Author of two books - Hacking Crux 1 & 2 - both on information security. He has been covered extensively by the global media and is one of the most followed Indian Information Security Expert on various social media platforms
